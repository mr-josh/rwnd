Guide

  1. Go to takeout.google.com and make sure the correct account is selected in the top right corner

  2. Click "Deselect All" then scroll down to the bottom and check "YouTube and YouTube Music"


  3. Click "Multiple Formats" and change the "History" format to "JSON". Then click OK.

  4. Click "All YouTube Data Included". Click "Deselect All" then check "History" only and click OK

  5. Click "Next Step" then Click "Create Export"

  6. Wait to get an email saying "Your export is ready for Download"

  7. Download and extract the zip file

  8. Open the "Takeout" folder and navigate to "YouTube and YouTube Music/history". Here you will find the watch-history.json file.

  9. Drag the watch-history.json file into the RWND file upload and voila!
